The Chicago Bulls are an American basketball team based out of Chicago Illinois. The teams
competes in the Eastern Conference and Central Division of the National Basketball Association (NBA).
The team was founded on Janurary 16th, 1966, and played their first game in the 1966-67 NBA season
After this season the Bulls would go on to be one of the most popular teams not only in the US, but the world.
The Chicago Bulls currently play their home games at the United Center, an arena on Chicago's West Side
shared with the Chicago Blackhawks of the National Hockey League (NHL).
